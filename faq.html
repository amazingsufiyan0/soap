Orders & Shipping

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days within the country. International shipping typically takes 7–14 business days depending on your location. Express shipping options are available at checkout for faster delivery.

Yes! We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $30. Orders under $30 attract a flat shipping fee of $5.99. Free shipping is automatically applied at checkout when your cart qualifies.

Returns & Refunds

We offer a 30-day return policy from the date of delivery. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Opened or used soaps cannot be returned for hygiene reasons, unless they arrived damaged or defective.

We're so sorry to hear that! Please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with your order number and a photo of the damaged item. We'll arrange a replacement or full refund at no additional cost to you.

Once your return has been received and inspected, refunds are processed within 5–7 business days. The funds will be returned to your original payment method. Credit card refunds may take an additional 3–5 business days to appear on your statement.

Products & Ingredients

Most of our soaps are gentle enough for sensitive skin, but everyone's skin is different. We recommend checking the full ingredient list for each product before purchasing. Our unscented and fragrance-free soaps are specially formulated for sensitive and reactive skin types.

Yes! All Ecosoap products are 100% vegan — we never use animal-derived ingredients. We are also fully cruelty-free and certified by The Vegan Society. We never test on animals and partner only with suppliers who share these values.

Our handmade soaps have a shelf life of 24 months from the date of manufacture. To maximise their life, keep them in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. When in use, let the soap dry fully between uses — a well-draining soap dish makes a big difference.

No. Our cold-process soap bars are naturally self-preserving due to the saponification process and their high pH. We do not add artificial preservatives. Our liquid products use a minimal amount of food-grade preservative to ensure product safety and longevity.
