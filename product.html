Our Activated Charcoal Handmade Soap is crafted in small batches using the cold-process method to preserve the integrity of all natural ingredients. Activated charcoal is a powerful natural ingredient known for its ability to attract and trap toxins from the skin's surface.

This bar is ideal for oily, acne-prone, or combination skin types. It provides a deep clean without stripping the skin of its natural moisture barrier. Each bar is hand-cut and slightly varies in weight and appearance — a natural characteristic of handmade soaps.
